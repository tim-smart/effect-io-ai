## DateTime.DateTime.UnitSingular

Singular date and time unit names used by rounding APIs such as
`DateTime.startOf`, `DateTime.endOf`, and `DateTime.nearest`.

**Signature**

```ts
type UnitSingular = | "millisecond"
    | "second"
    | "minute"
    | "hour"
    | "day"
    | "week"
    | "month"
    | "year"
```
